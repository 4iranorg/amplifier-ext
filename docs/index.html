Iran Amplifier

Amplify the voice of a nation

Privacy First, Always

Your data never touches our servers — because we don't have any.

No Servers

Your browser talks directly to the AI provider. No middlemen, no data collection.

No Tracking

Zero analytics, zero telemetry, zero surveillance. We don't even know you exist.

Your API Key

Stored locally on your device. Sent only to OpenAI or Anthropic. We can't access it.

Open Source

Every line of code is public. Verify, don't trust. Audit it yourself.

You Control the Message

Iran Amplifier is a tool that assists you — it never posts anything automatically. This extension does not automate posting, coordinate users, or generate identical messages.
